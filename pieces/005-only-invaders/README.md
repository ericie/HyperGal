# Only Invaders

A Space Invaders riff with the invaders removed.

Four defender cannons hold the top, right, bottom, and left edges of the
screen. They take turns sending bullets across the empty field, sometimes as
single shots and sometimes as sudden aggressive volleys. Each attack is
answered by the defender being targeted with a perfectly timed countershot that
catches it in midair.

The shots never miss, but the dancers are intentionally a little off-beat:
reaction times, intercept heights, aim points, lateral lunges, volley cadences,
and defensive urgency all vary. Some saves are clean and early. Others wait
until the last possible instant before erasing the incoming bullet.

Once in a while, a defender stops to charge and fires a larger super bullet. It
is still doomed to be caught, but when the countershot destroys it, the whole
screen flashes white and shakes under the blast. The blast clears every other
bullet from the field and resets all score counters to zero.

Every interception bursts into fireworks. Each defender's counter records how
many bullets it has erased.
