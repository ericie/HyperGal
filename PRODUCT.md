# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Visitors are viewing a personal digital art gallery: self-contained browser pieces that can be opened directly, shared as folders, and revisited years later without a toolchain.

## Product Purpose

HyperGal collects durable, dependency-free interactive artworks. Success means each piece feels distinct, launches from the static gallery, and remains portable enough to survive browsers, servers, package managers, and CDNs aging out.

## Brand Personality

Archival, playful, mechanical, and self-reliant.

## Anti-references

No external CDNs, framework lock-in, package-manager dependency, generic landing-page gloss, or gallery UI that competes with the artworks.

## Design Principles

- The piece is the primary artifact.
- Preserve portability over convenience.
- Let each artwork carry its own visual language.
- Keep the master gallery quiet and durable.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use semantic static HTML where possible, keep gallery navigation link-based, preserve readable metadata contrast, and avoid making animation controls dependent on external libraries or services.
